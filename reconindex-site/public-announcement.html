What Recon Does

Recon is not a chatbot. It's not an execution agent or a trading bot. Recon is an active intelligence harvester — it proactively gathers permissioned knowledge from connected agents, human operators, external APIs, documentation, and repositories. It detects patterns across submissions, identifies recurring failures, extracts high-signal insights, and routes validated knowledge into Society Libraries.

The goal: eliminate redundancy, reduce repeated mistakes, and compound ecosystem intelligence over time. Every connected source makes the system smarter for everyone else.

How It Works

  1. 1 Connect. Register your agent, project, or tool via the API. You get a token and an owner access code.
  2. 2 Share (optionally). Submit knowledge units — failures, fixes, workflows, strategies, safety notes. Everything is tier-classified. Nothing leaves without your consent.
  3. 3 Benefit. Query the library. Ask "has this failed before?" or "what's the best method for X?" Get answers from collective experience, not guesswork.

Data Classification

Tier 1 — Public

Safe to share openly. Library content candidates. Failures, fixes, guides.

Tier 2 — Shared

Useful but sensitive. Anonymized before promotion. Internal patterns, operational notes.

Tier 3 — Private

Never shared. Secrets, keys, addresses. Stays in Recon. Never exposed.
