## Overview
XRP.cafe is a community-run NFT marketplace and token discovery hub on the XRP Ledger. It serves as a "phone book" for the XRPL ecosystem, helping users find tokens, NFT collections, and projects. Unlike primary liquidity venues (e.g., xMagnetic or Sologenic), XRP.cafe focuses on aggregation and user-friendly discovery rather than deep orderbook trading.

## Key Features
- **NFT Marketplace**: Primary interface for browsing and trading XRPL-native NFTs (XLS-20).
- **Token Directory**: Lists popular tokens and their issuer addresses for easy DEX integration.
- **Community Hub**: Active presence in Discord/Telegram for grassroots ecosystem support.
